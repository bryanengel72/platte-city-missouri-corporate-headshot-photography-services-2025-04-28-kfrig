Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a headshot session take?

Our standard sessions typically last 60-90 minutes, allowing time for multiple outfit changes and various poses. We ensure you never feel rushed and capture the perfect shot.

What should I wear to my session?

We recommend solid colors and classic business attire that aligns with your industry. We'll provide detailed guidance before your session and welcome you to bring multiple options.

How quickly will I receive my photos?

You'll receive your professionally edited photos within 3-5 business days. Rush delivery is available for urgent needs at an additional fee.

Do you offer hair and makeup services?

Yes, we partner with professional stylists who specialize in camera-ready looks. This service can be added to any package for an additional fee.

What's included in your packages?

All packages include the photo session, professional retouching, multiple poses and expressions, and both high-resolution and web-optimized images.
